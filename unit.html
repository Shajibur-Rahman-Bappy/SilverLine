Our Business Units

From yarn to finished garments, we deliver excellence across the entire value chain.

Spinning

Our Spinning Division is equipped with the latest high-speed machinery, producing high-quality yarns for both local and international markets. We focus on efficiency, sustainability, and consistent quality.

  • Capacity: 200,000 spindles
  • Specialized in cotton, polyester, and blended yarns
  • Energy-efficient operations with green initiatives

Textiles

Our Textiles Division specializes in woven and knitted fabrics. With advanced dyeing, finishing, and printing technologies, we provide fabrics that meet the latest global fashion trends.

  • Annual production: 120 million meters of fabric
  • Modern dyeing & finishing units
  • Eco-friendly production using OEKO-TEX standards

Garments

Our Garments Division delivers top-quality apparel to leading global brands. With a skilled workforce, cutting-edge equipment, and compliance certifications, we ensure timely delivery and premium quality.

  • Capacity: 10 million pieces per year
  • Certified by WRAP, BSCI, OEKO-TEX, ISO
  • Exporting to USA, EU, and global markets
